Born in New Bern, North Carolina in 1893, Pepsi has grown from a small-town pharmacy creation into one of the world's most iconic beverage brands. Over 130 years of innovation have shaped every sip into a moment of pure refreshment.

Pepsi Classic stays true to its roots — the same bold cola flavour, the same unmistakable fizz, and the same commitment to quality that has made it a household name across more than 200 countries worldwide.

From ballpark stands to boardroom breaks, Pepsi Classic is there — the taste that connects generations and sparks moments of joy.

Fanta was born in 1940 in Germany, created by Max Keith who needed a new drink when Coca-Cola syrup couldn't cross the Atlantic during wartime. The name "Fanta" came from the German word "Fantasie" (imagination).

What started as a wartime necessity became a global sensation. Today, Fanta is enjoyed in over 190 countries, with more than 200 unique flavours — from classic Orange to exotic local varieties.

Every sip carries that original spirit of creativity and fun. Fanta isn't just a drink — it's an invitation to play, to imagine, to colour outside the lines.

FizzUp was born from a simple idea: what if a soft drink could make you feel like giving a thumbs up after every single sip? That spark of satisfaction became the foundation for an entirely new kind of lemon drink.

FizzUp Next is the latest evolution — a perfectly balanced carbonated lemon formula with real lemon juice concentrate and a fizz profile that dances on your tongue. No shortcuts, no compromises.

From school canteens to office coolers, FizzUp Next has quickly become the go-to choice for anyone who wants real fruit flavour, real refreshment, and a drink that genuinely earns that thumbs-up logo.

ColaNext was founded on a radical question: what would cola taste like if we started from scratch today? Not nostalgia — innovation. Not tradition — transformation.

Our team of flavour scientists spent two years developing a proprietary cola formula that delivers the deep, rich body you expect, but with a cleaner finish and a fizz profile that's noticeably smoother than legacy brands.

Every ColaNext can is made from 85% recycled aluminium. Our manufacturing runs on 100% renewable energy. We believe the next generation of cola shouldn't just taste better — it should be better, period.
